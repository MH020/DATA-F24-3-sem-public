All the Meow-tastic Info You Need

Welcome to our cat fact corner, where we celebrate curious felines and their fascinating quirks. From whisker wonders to purr-fect habits, we’ve got it all!

Did You Know?

Nap Masters

Cats can sleep up to 16 hours a day—that’s two-thirds of their entire life!

Communication Experts

Cats have over 100 different vocal sounds, while dogs only have about 10.

Whiskers Are Key

Whiskers help cats sense their surroundings and gauge tight spaces. They’re ultra sensitive!

Super Hearing

Cats can hear frequencies up to 64 kHz, well beyond what humans can detect.

Night Vision

A cat’s night vision is about six times better than that of a human, thanks to specialized eye cells.

Graceful Falls

Cats have a “righting reflex,” letting them twist mid-air to land on their feet.
